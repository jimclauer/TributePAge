Mr. Jim Clauer

The Church Guy

A timeline of Jim's work in the church world:

  • 1982 - Born to Roger and Eileen Clauer - Parents have him baptized, thus beginging his relationship with the church
  • 1994 - Begins playing piano for "Children's Liturgy of the Word" at St. William Church in Waukesha, WI
  • 1997 - Plays for the adult choir Christmas Concert and Masses
  • 2001 - begins studies at St. Norbert College in De Pere, WI
  • 2002 - Accepts the position of Coordinator of Music at St. Matthew Church in Allouez, WI
  • 2006 - Moves to Illinois in search of bigger churches and new opportunities. Becomes the Director of Liturgy and Music at Holy Family Parish in Shorewood, IL
  • 2009 - Becomes a Campus Minister at Joliet Catholic Academy in Joliet, IL
  • 2011 - Leaves the Diocese of Joliet for the Archdiocese of Chicago
  • 2018 - Returns to the Diocese of Joliet
  • 2021 - Begins working on learning Responsive Web Design course offered by FreeCodeCamp.org

Everytime I reflect on my past, I always think of what my grandmother told me before taking my driving test...

-- Jim Clauer
